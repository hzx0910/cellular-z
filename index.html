Cellular-Z PRIVACY POLICY

Thank you for useing Cellular-Z. Cellular-Z respects your privacy and takes your privacy safety seriously.

What information do we need?

We need information from you when you open Cellular-Z:

What do we use your information for?

All the information we collect from you will be displayed on your screen.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We only use your information offline( just in your cell phone ),we will never upload your information to any online server.
